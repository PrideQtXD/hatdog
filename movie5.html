Title:

Thor: Ragnarok

Release Date:

October 25, 2017

synopsis:

The powerful Thor, who is imprisoned on the opposite side of the cosmos, is forced to compete in a fatal gladiatorial match against the Hulk, another Avenger and his old comrade. Thor is in a race against time to stop the all-powerful Hela from eradicating the Asgardian civilization and his home Earth as part of his quest for survival.
